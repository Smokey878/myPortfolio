About Me

As an innovative and detail-oriented student at the University of Western Ontario, I am honing my skills in software engineering with a focus on full-stack development. Parallel to my academic journey, my passion for automotive engineering and crafting high-performance machines has been driven by self-directed learning and hands-on experience. Through numerous projects and personal initiatives, I continue to expand my expertise at the intersection of software and automotive technology.

More About Me

As an engineer who aspires to head into development and designing one day, my current focus is gaining experience in my fields of choice. As of now, I am chasing my passion of developing the new wave of automotive vehicles, an industry that is very uncertain in this day and age due to the shift from greenhouse fuels to a cleaner more renewable source of energy.

Towards this goal, I have realized I must start from the basics and make my way up. This is the reason why I have immersed myself with hands-on projects involving software development and automotive engineering.

My hope is that the knowledge I gain through my personal projects, as displayed in this portfolio (which actually also one of my own personally coded projects!) will help me improve and apply my increasing skill more effectively to the engineering industry.
